Privacy Policy

Minesweeper: Reboom · Effective date: July 15, 2026

Minesweeper: Reboom (the “App”) is a puzzle game. This policy explains what information is involved when you use the App. Our goal is to collect as little as possible.

1. Information the App collects

  • The App does not require an account and does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or other contact details.
  • Your gameplay data — settings, statistics, progress, achievements, and in-game coins — is stored on your device using standard iOS storage.
  • If you are signed in to iCloud, some of this data (statistics, achievements, coin balance, and campaign progress) is synced across your own devices through Apple’s iCloud key-value storage. This data is tied to your Apple ID and handled by Apple; the App has no separate server and cannot read it.

2. Third-party services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob (advertising). The App displays ads. Ads are requested as non-personalized — no cross-app tracking and no use of the device advertising identifier (IDFA). In the EEA, the UK, and Switzerland, a consent form is shown as required by Google’s User Messaging Platform. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Game Center (leaderboards and achievements), managed by Apple under your Apple ID.
  • Apple iCloud (cross-device sync described above).
  • Apple App Store / StoreKit (in-app purchases such as “Remove Ads”). Payments are processed by Apple; the App never receives your payment details.

3. Tracking

The App does not track you across other apps or websites and does not use the device advertising identifier for tracking. No App Tracking Transparency prompt is shown because no such tracking occurs.

4. Diagnostics

The App may write diagnostic logs on the device to help with debugging. These logs stay on your device and are not transmitted to us.

5. Children’s privacy

The App is suitable for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children, and advertising is served in non-personalized mode.

6. Data retention and deletion

On-device data is removed when you delete the App. iCloud-synced data can be managed or removed through your Apple ID and iCloud settings.

7. Your choices

  • Where applicable, you can review or reset your advertising consent through the in-app consent options or your device settings.
  • You can purchase “Remove Ads” to stop seeing advertisements.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Effective date” above.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy: minesweeper.reboom.support@gmail.com

This App is an independent work and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Microsoft or any other publisher of a game called “Minesweeper.”
